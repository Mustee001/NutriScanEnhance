About NutriScan AI

NutriScan AI is a smart assistant designed to help households and businesses reduce food waste through intelligent inventory management, aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger.

Our Mission: From Idea to Income

Our goal is to create a monetizable tool that makes a real-world impact. By providing users with AI-powered expiry detection based on actual purchase dates and storage conditions, realistic quantity tracking, intelligent recipe suggestions, and tangible impact metrics, we empower them to save food, save money, and contribute to a more sustainable future.

How It Works

  • Smart Input: Enter food name, purchase date, quantity, and storage conditions.
  • Intelligent Recognition: Our fuzzy matching algorithm handles misspellings and variations.
  • Realistic Calculations: Expiry dates calculated from actual purchase dates and storage science.
  • Quantity Tracking: Monitor portions and track usage over time.
  • Smart Notifications: Get browser alerts when items are about to expire.
  • Impact Analysis: Track financial savings and environmental impact with exportable reports.
  • Progressive Web App: Works offline and installs like a native mobile app.

Technical Features

  • Purchase Date-Based Expiry: Real calculations based on when you bought the item
  • Storage Condition Logic: Different shelf lives for fridge, freezer, pantry, and counter storage
  • Fuzzy String Matching: Levenshtein distance algorithm for intelligent food recognition
  • Quantity Management: Track weight, volume, and count with partial usage updates
  • Data Export: CSV export for analysis and PDF reports for sharing
  • Browser Notifications: Scheduled alerts for expiring items
  • Offline Functionality: Service worker for offline access and data synchronization
