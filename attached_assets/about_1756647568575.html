About NutriScan AI

NutriScan AI is a smart assistant designed to help households and businesses reduce food waste, aligning with UN Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger.

Our Mission: From Idea to Income

Our goal is to create a monetizable tool that makes a real-world impact. By providing users with AI-powered expiry detection, recipe suggestions, and tangible impact metrics, we empower them to save food, save money, and contribute to a more sustainable future.
